Abstract

This2 work, is a one reproduction on the paper finding surface Correspondence using symmetry axis curves. That paper propone an automatic algorithm for finding a correspondence map between two 3D surfaces. To that propose, using the global reflective symmetry axes, since are stable, recognizable, semantic features of most real-world surfaces. It is possible first extracting symmetry axis curves, aligning the extracted curves, and then extrapolating correspondences found on the curves to both surfaces. The main advantages of this approach are efficiency and robustness, for example we used optimal alignment of 1D curves with dynamic programming instead of mapping of two surfaces 2D.

Objetivo

Implementación de un algoritmo que encuentre la correspondencia entre dos superficies en 3D, esto es; dado dos mallas, M1 y M2 como entrada, debemos debemos encontrar la correspondencia de cada vértice de la malla M1 sobre la malla M2. Como trabajo adicional se hace uso de la GPU para realizar la ejecución de la parte más costosa de cálculo.
